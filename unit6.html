UNIT 6

Pytest and Test-Driven Development

Development Tema Project


The Development Team was provided with a transcript between two (hypothetical) characters in chapter 2 of the case study and Appendix 1 and 2.
The team is required to analyse the transcript and extract the initial requirements of both stakeholders and critique them with respect to assumptions and gaps in what is provided.
The team is required to produce a proposal that details a suitable development methodology (e.g. waterfall or agile based) for the system under consideration, supported by a discussion that justifies the choice of methodology.
